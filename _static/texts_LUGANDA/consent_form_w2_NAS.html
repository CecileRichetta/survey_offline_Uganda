Foomu y’Okukkiriza (Consent Form)

Gyebale ko, oli otya leero? Erinnya lyange ndi ENUMERATOR_NAME. Nkola n’ekitongole ky’okunoonyereza ekitali kya byabufuzi ekiyitibwa Matrice360 Uganda. Mu nkolagana n’abanoonyereza okuva ku Yunivasite y’e Geneva, tukola okunoonyereza okw’ebyafaayo ku mirembe n’entalo mu Uganda, okugeza entalo za Lord’s Resistance Army (LRA) ezalwana n’obwakabaka bwa Gavumenti ya Uganda mu Northern Uganda.

Emabega watuukiriza omugongo ogusooka ogw’okunoonyereza kwaffe: tukwebaza nnyo olw’okwetaba. Leero kino kye kitundu ekyokubiri eky’okunoonyereza.

Okunoonyereza kuno kuli mu nkolagana y’ensi ez’enjawulo ey’okunoonyereza ekulembeddwamu Prof. Simon Hug ku Yunivasite y’e Geneva, Switzerland, mu nkolagana ne Matrice360 Uganda ekulembeddwamu Bwana Samuel. Okunoonyereza okufaanana nakwo kukolebwa ne mu Colombia, Turkey ne Thailand.

Ekigendererwa ekikulu eky’okunoonyereza kuno kwe kutegeera bulungi engeri entalo gye zikyusaamu obulamu bw’abantu mu mbeera y’embeera y’abantu (social wellbeing), eri abo abaakwatibwa entalo mu ngeri ey’obutereevu oba mu ngeri etali ya butereevu, n’engeri gye bayinza okuyingizibwa obulungi mu kitundu mwebabeera. Ebivudde mu kunoonyereza bino bijja kuyamba okuteekateeka enteekateeka n’amateeka g’omu biseera eby’omu maaso ag’okuddamu okuzimba n’okuyingiza mu bulamu obw’awamu abo abaakosebwa entalo ng’ezo.

Walonze mu ngeri ya kalonda (random) mu kyalo kino ng’omukiikirira ekitundu kino. Ojja kubuuzibwa ebibuuzo ebiwerako era ojja kwetaba mu bikolwa bitono, nga muno mujja kusobola okufuna ssente entono. Tewali bibuuzo bituufu oba ebitali bituufu; ekiddamu ekisinga obulungi kye kiraga endowooza yo mu ngeri entuufu. Ekitundu kino kijja kutwala eddakiika nga 45. Ng’akabonero k’okwebaza obudde n’okufuba kwo, ojja kufuna 10,000-UGX, awamu n’ensimbi endala eziva mu bikolwa ebitono by’ojja okwetabamu.

Ku lw’ekigendererwa ky’okunoonyereza kwaffe, tujja kubuuza ebibuuzo ebikwata ku bintu by’oyitamu mu biseera by’entalo, n’endowooza zo ku bitundu ebyali by’olemesa (opposing groups), naye ebiddamu byo byonna bijja kukuumibwa mu kyama.

Ekitundu kino kya kubuuza bibuuzo (questionnaire) tekirina linnya: ebiddamu byo bikuumibwa mu mutindo ogw’obukuumi era nga byawuliddwa ku mawulire go ag’okukukwatako, ku siva (server) erina obukuumi ku Yunivasite y’e Geneva, okusobozesa okutangira okuddamu okutegeerwa (re-identification). Bijja kukozesebwa mu ngeri etategeeza muntu yenna mu kunoonyereza okw’abanoonyereza abakiriziddwa.

Olina eddembe okulekera awo okwetaba mu kunoonyereza essaawa yonna era ku nsonga yonna, nga tewali bivaamu bibi. Tewali bulabe bukulu busuubirwa. Bw’oba oweraliikirira oba obadde otabuse mu mbeera embi mu kiseera ky’okunoonyereza oba oluvannyuma lwakyo, tukusaba otutegeeze amangu ddala.
