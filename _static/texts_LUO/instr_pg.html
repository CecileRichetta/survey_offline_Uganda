• Yin ki lawoti wutye ki mic angwen
  • Yin ibi moko tami ikom mic adi ma imito gwoko ne piri ki mic adi ma imito keto ne i agulu alwak
  • Iromo yero me miyo mic kamokeken nicake ki 0 me oo 4 iyi agulu alwak
  • Pi mic acel acel ma igwoko, ibi nongo mic acel i agiki me leyo tam man
  • Wel mic ma yin ki lawoti ni wu keto i agulu alwak kibi ribo weng ci lapwony obi pongo nucu pa mic meno
  • Ibi nongo nucu pa mic matye i agulu alwak inge lapwony opongo ne
  • Wel mic cumuni weng ma ibi nongo obi bedo rom ki wel mic ma igwoko med ki ( wel mic ma yin ki lawoti oketo iyi agulu med ki wel nucu pa mic ma lapwony omedo) pok ki aryo
