  • In ibi moko tami ikom mic adi ma imito gwoko ne piri ki mic adi ma imito keto ne i agulu alwak
  • Iromo yero me keto mic kamo keken nicake ki i jero me oo i angwen iyi agulu alwak
  • Pi mic acel acel ma igwoko, ibi nongo mic acel i agiki me leyo tam eni.
  • Wel mic ma in ki lawoti ni wu keto iyi agulu alwak kibi ribo weng ci lapwony/ lakwed man obi pongo wiye ki nucu pa wel meno
  • Ibi nongo nucu pa wel ma tye iyi agulu alwak i nge lapwony/ lakwed opongo wiye.
  • Wel mic weng ma ibi nongo obi bedo rom ki wel mic ma igwoko med ki ( wel mic ma in ki lawoti oketo iyi agulu med ki wel nucu pa mic ma lapwony/lakwed omedo) pok ki aryo.
