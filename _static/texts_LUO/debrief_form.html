Lok me agiki

Apwoyo me bedo leyo tam man. Ikine me bedo ileyo tam man, Ngec mogo onongo ki kano woko me neno ni leyo tam man ocung ikom ada. Kombedi dong ma itykeo leyo tam, wa romo tito boti ngec ma onongo ki kano woko ni ki me tito ne kore ki kore.



1. Incomplete Disclosure: I leyo tam man man, ibedo igin ma ki tye ka temo ne katiyo ma dok ilok kom tugu cwiny ma tutwale ki yubu me tugu cwiny ikom gen. Romo bedi ikato ki iboko lok ma ki tamo atama onyo coc ma ki yubu ki cwiny me kelo gen iyi adam. Nyik lok man weng obedo gin ma ki tamo atama ki kitiyo kwede me ngiyo kit ma gen loko kwede kit me kwo ka kama ki gure idul.



2. Why This was Necessary: Kit me tuku ki adam me kelo gen pire obedo tek tutwal i kit me yubu leyo tam man. Tyen loke obedo me niang kit ma cwiny me gen ka ki tugu loko kwede kit me kwo iyi ka gure macalo dul, tero dano marom aroma, ki gen. Ka onong jo ma gu bedo ileyo tam man gu ngeyo tyen lok me kwan man kikome, lagam ma megi onongo romo loke woko, kit meno nongo balo woko ada ki ngec man me bedo gin ma gene. Yoo ma ki ityo kwede me leyo tam man weko wa kato ki awogi matir ikom tic ma gen timo me kelo ribe ki ngo ma dano timo me konyo dano mukene.



3. Full Disclosure of The Study: Neno ma pire tek ikwan man obedo me niang kace cuku cwiny ikom gen romo ilo ngo ma dano timo me konyo dano mukene, tutwale kama nongo ribe ikin dule mapat pat ogure iye mite. Tic atima ki tam ma i moko ikare me tuku ma ki tye ka temo ne ni okonyo wa me pimo kit ma gen romo loko kit me kwo ikom te kare ma tye ada. Adwogi ma aa ki ikwan man bi konyo niang kwan madit kit ma tamme cwiny romo kelo alokaloka i ribe ki yoo ma ki tiyo kwede me cobo ayela yela.



Wa lego ni pe inywak ngo ma ibedo ka timo ne ki neno me leyo tam man ki ngat mo keken ma ca gwok romo bedo ileyo tam man iyi anyim, pien man romo balo kit leyo tam man matye ka wot anyimi.
