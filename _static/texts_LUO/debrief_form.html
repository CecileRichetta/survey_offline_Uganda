Lok me agiki

Apwoyo me bedo i leyo tam man. Ikine me bedo ileyo tam man, Ngec mogo onongo ki kano woko me neno ni coko ngec man ocung ikom ada. Kombedi dong ma ityeko leyo tam man, wa romo tito boti ngec ma onongo ki kano woko ni ki me tito ne kore ki kore.



1. Incomplete Disclosure: I leyo tam man, ibedo igin ma ki tye ka temo ne katiyo madok ilok kom tugu cwiny ma tutwale ki yubu me tugu cwiny ikom gen. Romo bedoni ikato ki iboko lok ma ki tamo atama onyo coc ma ki yubu ki cwiny me kelo gen iyi adam. Nyig lok man weng obedo gin ma ki tamo atama ki kitiyo kwede me ngiyo kit ma gen loko kwede kit me kwo ka kama ki gure ki idul.



2. Why This was Necessary: Kit me tuku ki adam me kelo gen pire obedo tek tutwal i kit me yubu kwed man. Tyen loke obedo me niang kit ma cwiny me gen ka ki tugu loko kwede kit me kwo iyi ka gure macalo dul, tero dano marom aroma, ki gen. Ka onongo jo ma gu bedo i leyo tam man gu ngeyo tyen lok me leyo tam man kikome, lagam ma megi onongo romo loke woko, kit meno nongo balo woko ada ki ngec man me bedo gin ma gene. Yoo ma ki ityo kwede me leyo tam man weko wa kato ki adwogi matir ikom tic ma gen timo me kelo ribe ki ngo ma dano timo me konyo dano mukene.



3. Full Disclosure of The Study: Neno ma pire tek i leyo tam man obedo me niang kace cuku cwiny ikom gen romo ilo ngo ma dano timo me konyo dano mukene, tutwale kama nongo ribe ikin dul mapat pat ma ogure mite. Tic atima ki tam ma i moko ikare me tuku ma ki tye ka temo ne ni okonyo wa me pimo kit ma gen romo loko kit me kwo ikom te kare ma tye ada. Adwogi ma aa ki i leyo tam man bi konyo niang kwed madit kit ma tam me cwiny romo kelo alokaloka i ribe ki yoo ma ki tiyo kwede me cobo ayela yela.



Wa lego ni pe inyak ngo ma ibedo ka timo ne ki neno me kwan man ki ngat mo keken ma ca gwok romo bedo ikwan man iyi anyim, pien man romo balo kit kwan man matye ka wot anyimi.
