  • In this game, the system automatically matches "Proposer" and "Receiver."
  • You play as the Receiver (Orange person in the examples).
  • The Proposer has 4 tokens.
  • You as the Receivers do not initially receive any tokens.
  • You propose the number of tokens that you want to get, from 0 to 4 tokens.
  • If the Proposer gives you fewer tokens than you requested, you receive no tokens.
  • If the Proposer gives you more tokens than you requested, you receive the number of tokens the Proposer offered.
