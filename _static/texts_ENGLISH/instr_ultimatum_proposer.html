• In this game, the system automatically matches "Proposer" and "Receiver".
  • You play as the Proposer (Blue person in the examples).
  • We give you 4 tokens.
  • The Receiver does not receive any tokens initially.
  • You have to offer the numbers of tokens that you are willing to give, from 0 to 4 tokens.
  • If you give the Receiver fewer tokens than they ask for, you will not receive any tokens.
  • If you give the Receiver more tokens than they ask for, the Receiver will receive the number of tokens offered.
