Please read the summary of the report from the United Nations Human Rights Council carefully.


Opportunities for Peace and Reconciliation in Uganda


An international research team consisting of researchers and experts on ethnic conflict have examined various scenarios regarding the future of the conflict in Northern Uganda. They concluded that reconciliation efforts like the ones in place in Uganda are helping former combatants reintegrate into their communities and contribute to rebuilding areas affected by the conflict. Hence, they are optimistic that with these efforts a lasting peace in Northern Uganda is possible.
