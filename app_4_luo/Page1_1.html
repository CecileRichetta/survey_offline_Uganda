Gum pi kuc ki mato oput I Uganda:


Dul pa Lukwed madongo I rwom me wilobo kacel ki Ludiro I ngec makwako lweny/tele I kin kaki ma pat pat malube ki lweny ma obedo I Kumalo me Uganda . Gin gu moko ni yoo ma pat pat me ribo dano calo mogo madong tye katime I Uganda labole mato oput ni tye ka konyo ribo lulweny macon I kin gangi ki bene tye ka konyo me yubu kabedo ma lweny man ogudu.Pi meno , gin tye gi gen ki tek cwiny ni kuc mari pi naka I Kumalo me Uganda dong twere.
